Vismaya Hall

"Elegance & Versatility"

Welcome to Vismaya Hall, nestled within the esteemed Anns Convention Centre in Kadapra Mannar, Pathanamthitta. As you explore the options for hosting your special events, we aim to provide comprehensive insights into the unique attributes and offerings of Vismaya Hall. Vismaya Hall is meticulously designed to offer an intimate and elegant ambiance, perfect for hosting smaller-scale events. With a capacity to accommodate up to 75 guests, this hall creates an inviting atmosphere conducive to fostering meaningful connections and cherished memories. The hallmark of Vismaya Hall lies in its versatility. Whether you're planning an engagement ceremony, a family reunion, a corporate meeting, or a small-scale celebration, this hall can be tailored to suit your specific requirements. At Anns Convention Centre, guest comfort is paramount. Vismaya Hall is equipped with modern amenities, including full air-conditioning, ensuring a comfortable environment for all attendees, irrespective of external weather conditions. Additionally, ample parking space located conveniently at the rear of the venue ensures hassle-free parking and seamless ingress and egress for guests. Our dedicated team at Anns Convention Centre is committed to providing personalized assistance every step of the way. From initial inquiries to event execution, our experienced staff members are on hand to address your queries, accommodate your requests, and ensure a seamless and memorable experience for you and your guests. Vismaya Hall offers easy accessibility from Thiruvalla, Mavelikara, Chengannur, Harippad, Pulikeezhu, Edathua, Pandanadu, Othara, Kallissery, and surrounding areas.
