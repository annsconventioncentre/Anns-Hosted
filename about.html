About Us

who we are

Anns Convention Centre Your premier venue for exceptional events, blending luxury and convenience seamlessly. Experience unforgettable celebrations with our versatile halls and dedicated service.

Welcome to Anns Convention Centre, where we turn ordinary events into extraordinary experiences. Nestled in the heart of Kerala, our convention center stands as a beacon of elegance and hospitality, providing a premier venue for weddings, receptions, engagement functions, birthday parties, and other special occasions.
Mr. Shaji P John - founder
Established with a vision to redefine event hosting, Anns Convention Centre has been a cornerstone of celebrations in the region for five years. Our journey began with a passion for creating memorable moments and a commitment to excellence in service. At Anns, our mission is simple yet profound: to exceed the expectations of every guest who walks through our doors. We strive to provide unparalleled hospitality, luxurious amenities, and personalized attention to ensure that every event hosted here is nothing short of perfection. We uphold the highest standards of quality in everything we do, from our facilities to our customer service. Hospitality is at the core of our ethos. We treat every guest with warmth, respect, and genuine care. We continuously innovate and adapt to meet the evolving needs of our clients, ensuring that every event is unique and memorable. Behind every successful event at Anns Convention Centre is a dedicated team of professionals who are passionate about hospitality and committed to exceeding expectations. From our event planners to our culinary experts and support staff, every member of our team plays a crucial role in bringing your vision to life.
